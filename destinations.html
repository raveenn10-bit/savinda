Know Before You Go

Everything you need to know about each destination — places to visit, health considerations and local guide availability.

Maldives

Medical & Health

No mandatory vaccinations required. Resorts have on site medical staff; serious cases are transferred to Male by speedboat or seaplane. Reef-safe sunscreen recommended.

Local Guides

We provide local resort guides who can accompany you throughout your stay, including for snorkelling trips, island excursions and water sport activities. Multilingual guides can be arranged on request for travellers who prefer a language other than English.

Dubai

Medical & Health

No mandatory vaccinations. High standard of healthcare with modern hospitals in the city. Travel insurance recommended for non-residents.

Local Guides

Our licensed local guides can travel with you for city tours and desert safaris, sharing insider knowledge of the destination. If you're travelling in a group that prefers Arabic or another language, we can arrange a guide who speaks it.

Bali

Medical & Health

Hepatitis A and Typhoid vaccinations are often recommended. Drink bottled water. Clinics are widely available in tourist areas.

Local Guides

Local trekking and cultural guides are available to join you for volcano sunrise hikes and temple visits. We can also arrange guides in Indonesian or other languages if that suits your group better.

Thailand

Medical & Health

Hepatitis A and Typhoid vaccinations often recommended. Good hospital network in Bangkok and Phuket. Avoid tap water.

Local Guides

We can provide local guides to accompany you on temple tours, island hopping trips and food experiences. If English isn't your first language, just let us know and we'll arrange a guide who speaks yours.

Switzerland

Medical & Health

No special vaccinations required. Excellent healthcare system. Travel insurance with mountain rescue cover is recommended for hiking trips.

Local Guides

Certified mountain guides are available to accompany you on alpine hikes for added safety and local insight. Guides who speak German or other languages can be arranged for travellers who prefer it.

Paris

Medical & Health

No special vaccinations required. High standard public and private healthcare. EU travel insurance or international cover recommended.

Local Guides

Our local guides can join you for museum visits and walking tours around the city. If you'd feel more comfortable with a French speaking guide, or any other language, we can arrange that for your trip.

Singapore

Medical & Health

No mandatory vaccinations. Excellent healthcare system, considered one of the best in Asia. Tap water is safe to drink.

Local Guides

Local guides are available to accompany your family or group around the city's attractions and food trails. We can also provide guides in different languages depending on your group's needs.

Malaysia

Medical & Health

Hepatitis A vaccination often recommended. Good hospital network in Kuala Lumpur. Drink bottled water outside major cities.

Local Guides

We can arrange local guides to travel with you for city tours, cable car trips and island excursions. If you would prefer a guide who speaks Malay or another language, just let us know when booking.
