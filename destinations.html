Know Before You Go

Crucial travel information compiled by our destination specialists covering medical and local guidance guidelines.

Maldives

Medical & Health

No mandatory vaccinations required. Resorts have on-site medical staff; serious cases are transferred to Male by speedboat or seaplane. Reef-safe sunscreen recommended.

Local Guides

We provide local resort guides who can accompany you throughout your stay, including for snorkeling trips, island excursions and water sport activities. Multilingual guides can be arranged on request.

Dubai

Medical & Health

No mandatory vaccinations. High standard of healthcare with modern hospitals in the city. Travel insurance recommended for non-residents.

Local Guides

Our licensed local guides can travel with you for city tours and desert safaris, sharing insider knowledge of the destination. Multilingual guides available upon request.

Bali

Medical & Health

Hepatitis A and Typhoid vaccinations are often recommended. Drink bottled water. Modern clinics are widely available in main tourist hubs like Seminyak, Kuta, and Ubud.

Local Guides

Local trekking and cultural guides are available to join you for volcano sunrise hikes and temple visits. Private driver-guides can be arranged for custom routes.

Thailand

Medical & Health

Hepatitis A and Typhoid vaccinations often recommended. Good hospital network in Bangkok and Phuket. Avoid tap water and opt for bottled mineral water.

Local Guides

We provide licensed local guides for historical temple tours in Ayutthaya, island hopping trips in Phi Phi, and street food excursions. Language-specific guides are bookable.

Switzerland

Medical & Health

No special vaccinations required. Excellent healthcare system. Travel insurance with mountain rescue coverage is highly recommended for alpine sports and trekking.

Local Guides

Certified alpine mountain guides are available to accompany you on high-altitude hikes for safety and geographical context. Multilingual guides easily arranged.

Paris

Medical & Health

No special vaccinations required. High standard public and private healthcare. EU health insurance cards or international coverage recommended.

Local Guides

Expert local art historians and guides are available for museum walking tours, Louvre private tours, and historical city walking tours.

Singapore

Medical & Health

No mandatory vaccinations. World-class healthcare system. Tap water is perfectly safe and treated to WHO standards. Carry hydration in the humid climate.

Local Guides

Local guides are available to accompany your family or corporate group around biophilic city gardens, cultural ethnic quarters, and culinary heritage trails.

Malaysia

Medical & Health

Hepatitis A vaccination often recommended. Excellent hospital networks in Kuala Lumpur and Penang. Drink bottled water outside major metropolitan areas.

Local Guides

We arrange local guides for heritage walks in Penang, cable car tours in Genting Highlands, and nature treks in Langkawi. Guides fluent in multiple languages are bookable.
