Our History

ENIX Travels was founded with a simple goal: to make world class travel planning accessible to everyone. What began as a small team helping friends and family plan their holidays has grown into a trusted travel agency serving clients across the region. Over the years we have built strong relationships with hotels, airlines and local guides across our partner destinations, allowing us to design trips that are both affordable and unforgettable.

Our Story

Every great journey begins with a single story, and ours started with a simple belief: that travel has the power to change how we see the world, and ourselves. What began as a handful of carefully planned trips for friends and family has grown into ENIX Travels, a name trusted by travellers seeking more than just a destination, an experience worth remembering.

Over the years, we have walked alongside thousands of travellers, from honeymooners chasing sunsets in the Maldives to families exploring the wonders of Singapore, and adventurers tracing alpine trails in Switzerland. Each journey has shaped who we are today, a team driven not by routine, but by genuine care for every story we help write.

Today, ENIX Travels stands as a bridge between dreams and departures, blending world class partnerships, local expertise and a personal touch into every itinerary we craft. Our story is still being written, one journey, one traveller and one unforgettable memory at a time.

Terms & Conditions

Please review the following terms before booking a trip with ENIX Travels. By confirming a booking, you agree to the conditions outlined below.

📝 Booking & Payment

A booking is confirmed once the required details are submitted through our booking form and accepted by our team. Full or partial payment may be required at the time of booking, depending on the package and travel date.

❌ Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made 30 days or more before the trip start date are eligible for a 100% refund. Cancellations made less than 30 days before the trip start date are subject to a 5% cancellation fee, with the remaining 95% refunded to the traveller.

⚖ Traveller Conduct & Liability

ENIX Travels is not responsible for the personal conduct of travellers while abroad. Any consequences arising from a traveller's own actions or behaviour in a destination country are the sole responsibility of that traveller.

🌍 Local Laws & Regulations

All travellers must respect and follow the local laws, customs and regulations of each destination country visited as part of their trip. Failure to comply may result in consequences imposed by local authorities, for which ENIX Travels holds no liability.

🧳 Changes to Itinerary

While we make every effort to deliver the itinerary as planned, certain elements such as flights, hotels or activities may be subject to change due to circumstances beyond our control, including weather or supplier availability.

🛡 Travel Insurance

Travellers are strongly encouraged to obtain appropriate travel insurance for their trip. ENIX Travels can provide guidance on this but does not provide insurance coverage directly.

For full details about our terms and conditions, or if you have any questions before booking, please get in touch with our team.
