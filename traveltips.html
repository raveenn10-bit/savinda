Travel Tips & FAQ

Helpful advice for your trip and answers to the questions we hear most from our travellers.

Travel Tips

A few simple things to keep in mind before and during your trip, wherever you're headed.

🧳 Pack Smart

Check the weather for your destination and pack light, versatile clothing. Always carry a copy of your passport and travel documents separately from the originals.

📅 Book Early

Popular destinations and peak seasons fill up fast. Booking your flights and accommodation a few months ahead usually gets you better prices and more availability.

🛂 Check Visa Requirements

Visa rules vary by destination and can change. Check our Visa Requirement Summary on the Destinations page well ahead of your travel date.

💉 Health Preparations

Some destinations recommend specific vaccinations or health precautions. Check the Medical & Health notes on our Destinations page and consult your doctor if needed.

🛡 Get Travel Insurance

A good travel insurance policy protects you against unexpected medical costs, cancellations and lost luggage. We can help you choose the right cover.

💱 Manage Your Money

Carry a mix of cash and cards, notify your bank of international travel, and check the local currency exchange rate before you go.

🔌 Check Power Adapters

Plug types and voltage vary by country. Bring the right adapter or a universal travel adapter to keep your devices charged.

📱 Stay Connected

Look into a local SIM card or international roaming plan ahead of time so you can stay in touch and access maps and translation apps easily.

Frequently Asked Questions

The questions our customers ask most often.

1. How do I book a travel package with ENIX Travels?

Browse our Packages page, choose the package that suits you, and click "Book Now". This takes you to our Contact page where you can fill in your details and our team will follow up to confirm your booking.

2. Can I customize an existing package?

Yes. On our Contact page you can select "I want to Customize My Own Package" and tell us the countries, activities and any special requests you have, and we will design a plan around them.

3. What is included in the price shown for each package?

Each package listing shows what is included, such as accommodation, meals and transfers. Flights, visas and personal expenses are generally arranged separately unless stated otherwise.

4. Do you help with visa applications?

Yes, we offer visa consultation and guidance on required documents and the application process for most of our destinations. Visit our About page to learn more about this service.

5. How far in advance should I book my trip?

We recommend booking at least 2 to 3 months ahead for most destinations, and earlier for peak season travel or multi-country custom trips.

6. What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellation terms vary depending on the hotels and airlines involved in your package. Our team will explain the specific policy for your booking before you confirm.

7. Can I modify my booking after it is confirmed?

Yes, you can request changes through our Contact page by selecting "Modify Existing Booking" as your purpose of inquiry, and our team will assist you.

8. Do you arrange airport transfers?

Many of our packages include airport transfers. You can also request this separately through our booking form if it is not already included.

9. Is travel insurance required for my trip?

It is not always mandatory, but we strongly recommend it for every trip. You can request travel insurance guidance directly on our booking form.

10. How can I contact ENIX Travels for more help?

You can reach us through our Contact page by phone, email or our booking form, or message us directly on our social media pages linked in the footer.
