Our Legacy & History

ENIX Travels was founded with a simple goal: to make world-class travel planning accessible to everyone. What began as a small team helping friends and family plan their holidays has grown into a trusted travel agency serving clients across the region. Over the years, we have built strong relationships with hotels, airlines, and local guides across our partner destinations, allowing us to design trips that are both affordable and unforgettable.
